This Privacy Policy explains how Scrapbook ("Scrapbook," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the Scrapbook mobile application and related services collectively referred to as the "App."

Scrapbook is owned and operated by Ashton Searcy. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you create and use a Scrapbook account:

  • Name;
  • Username;
  • Email address;
  • Optional profile photo;
  • Content you post, upload, or share, including images, text, and audio;
  • Comments, likes, shares, and other interactions;
  • Messages sent through the App.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

2. Information You Choose to Provide

You may choose to provide content and information when you:

  • Create or update your profile;
  • Upload photos or other content;
  • Add text or audio to posts;
  • Comment on posts;
  • Like or share posts;
  • Send messages;
  • Report content or users;
  • Contact us for support.

Posts and profile content on Scrapbook are public.

3. Camera and Photo Library Access

Scrapbook may request access to your camera and photo library so you can create, upload, and share content. We only access your camera or photo library when you give permission through your device settings. You can change or revoke these permissions at any time in your device settings.

4. Information We Do Not Collect

Based on our current app functionality, Scrapbook does not collect:

  • Precise location;
  • Approximate location;
  • Contacts;
  • Phone number;
  • Birthday;
  • Payment information;
  • Advertising identifiers for tracking;
  • Analytics data;
  • Crash reporting data.

Scrapbook does not currently use third-party advertising networks, analytics tools, or payment processors.

5. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage user accounts;
  • Allow users to post, customize, and share content;
  • Display profiles, posts, comments, likes, shares, and messages;
  • Enable social features;
  • Support reporting and blocking features;
  • Review reports and moderate content;
  • Use keyword filters to help identify prohibited content;
  • Respond to support requests;
  • Maintain the safety and integrity of the App;
  • Enforce our Terms of Service;
  • Comply with applicable law.

6. Public Information

Scrapbook is a public social media app. Your profile and posts are public by default. Other users may be able to view your username, name, profile photo, posts, comments, likes, shares, and other public activity. Do not post personal information or content that you do not want others to see.

7. Messages

Scrapbook allows users to send messages. Messages are not end-to-end encrypted. We may access or review messages when necessary for safety, moderation, support, security, legal compliance, or enforcement of our Terms of Service.

8. Moderation and Safety

Scrapbook allows users to report content and block other users. We manually review reports and use keyword filters to help identify certain types of prohibited content. We may remove content, restrict access, suspend accounts, or terminate accounts if we believe a user has violated our Terms of Service or created a safety risk.

9. How We Store Information

Scrapbook uses Supabase to help store and operate account information, user content, and app data. Your information may be stored and processed by Supabase or related infrastructure providers that help operate the App.

10. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your personal information with advertisers. We may share information only in limited circumstances, such as:

  • With service providers that help us operate the App, such as Supabase;
  • When necessary to comply with law, legal process, or government requests;
  • To protect the safety, rights, or property of Scrapbook, users, or others;
  • To enforce our Terms of Service;
  • In connection with a business transfer, merger, acquisition, or similar event.

11. Data Retention

We keep your account information and content while your account is active. When you delete your account through your profile in the App, your account and associated data are deleted from Scrapbook's active records, unless retention is required for legal, security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or other legitimate reasons. We do not retain deleted account data for separate backup or legal retention purposes.

12. Account and Data Deletion

You may delete your account and request deletion of your data directly in the App through your profile. To delete your account:

  1. Open Scrapbook;
  2. Go to your profile;
  3. Open the settings menu;
  4. Select the account deletion option;
  5. Confirm deletion.

You may also contact us with privacy or deletion questions at: ashton.searcy@icloud.com.

13. Children's Privacy

Scrapbook is intended for users who are at least 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take reasonable steps to delete that information.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through Scrapbook, contact us at: ashton.searcy@icloud.com.

14. Your Choices

You may:

  • Update your profile information in the App;
  • Delete your account through your profile;
  • Stop posting public content;
  • Block other users;
  • Report content or users;
  • Change camera and photo library permissions through your device settings;
  • Contact us with privacy questions.

15. Security

We use reasonable measures designed to protect information. However, no app, website, server, or internet transmission is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

16. International Users

Scrapbook is operated from the United States. If you use the App from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the United States or other locations where our service providers operate.

17. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we may notify users through the App or by other reasonable means. Your continued use of Scrapbook after the updated Privacy Policy becomes effective means you accept the updated policy.

18. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal information, contact us at: ashton.searcy@icloud.com.
